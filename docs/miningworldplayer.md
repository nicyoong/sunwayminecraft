# Mining World / Living World

## What this feature is

Sunway has two main survival worlds with different purposes:

- **Mining World** is the resettable world for gathering resources and exploring.
- **Life World** is the persistent world for living, building, and long-term settlement.

Use the Mining World when you want ores, stone, wood, and exploration. Use the Life World when you want a stable place to return to.

---

## What each world is for

### Mining World
The Mining World exists so players can gather resources in a world that staff can reset when needed.

Use it for:
- mining
- gathering materials
- exploration
- short-term trips for resources

Do **not** use it for:
- permanent homes
- permanent builds
- long-term storage
- anything you expect to survive a reset

### Life World
The Life World is the stable world for normal living and building.

Use it for:
- homes
- towns
- builds you want to keep
- regular long-term play

---

## Commands

### `/mineworld`
Sends you to the Mining World.

Use this when you want to go resource gathering or exploring.

### `/lifeworld`
Returns you to the Life World.

This sends you:
1. to your personal spawn point, if you have one
2. otherwise to the Life World spawn

### `/mininginfo`
Shows the purpose, rules, and current status of the Mining World.

Use this if you want to check whether the Mining World is open, reset pending, or locked.

---

## Mining World status

The Mining World has three possible states.

### OPEN
The Mining World is available.

You can enter it normally with `/mineworld`.

### RESET PENDING
The Mining World is still open, but staff has marked it for closure or reset soon.

Important:
- you can still enter during this state
- you may see warning messages
- you should not start anything you are not ready to lose or leave behind

### LOCKED
The Mining World is closed.

You cannot enter it until staff reopens it.

---

## How travel works

### Going to Mining World
When you use `/mineworld`, you are sent to the Mining World spawn.

If the Mining World is locked, you will not be allowed in.

### Returning to Life World
When you use `/lifeworld`, the server tries to send you to your personal spawn point first.

If you do not have a personal spawn point, it sends you to the Life World spawn instead.

---

## What happens during evacuation

Before the Mining World is closed, staff may start an evacuation countdown.

During evacuation:
- the server announces that the Mining World will close soon
- warning messages may appear at countdown intervals
- players in the Mining World should leave as soon as possible

When the countdown finishes:
- everyone still in the Mining World is forced out
- players are returned out of the Mining World
- the Mining World becomes locked

---

## Rules

1. The Mining World is for gathering and exploration.
2. Do not make the Mining World your permanent home.
3. Do not rely on the Mining World for long-term storage.
4. Assume that the Mining World can be closed and reset.
5. If the world is marked reset pending, wrap up what you are doing and be ready to leave.
6. Use the Life World for anything permanent.

---

## Tips

- Keep valuable long-term items in the Life World, not the Mining World.
- Check `/mininginfo` before starting a long resource trip.
- If you want a guaranteed stable return point, set your spawn properly in the Life World.
- If staff announces evacuation, leave early instead of waiting for the final teleport.

---

## FAQ

### Can I build in the Mining World?
You technically can place things there, but you should not treat it as a permanent place to live or store important items. The world is meant to reset.

### Can I mine in the Life World?
Yes, but the Mining World is the world specifically intended for large-scale resource gathering.

### Why was I kicked out of the Mining World?
Most likely the Mining World evacuation finished and the world was locked.

### Why did `/lifeworld` not send me to my bed or house?
If you do not have a valid personal respawn point, the server sends you to the Life World spawn instead.

### Can I still go to Mining World when it says reset pending?
Yes. Reset pending is a warning state, not a lock state. But you should expect closure soon.

### What should I do if the Mining World is locked?
Wait for staff to reopen it. Use the Life World in the meantime.
